I built a 3-page interactive Power BI dashboard. In this Power BI project, I analyzed a €1.02B total pipeline with 12K+ deals to uncover key insights across regions, industries, products, and sales stages.
🔎 Key Insights from the Report:
•	📊 Overall Win Rate: 25.52% with €241M Won Revenue
•	🌍 Europe leads in total annual revenue contribution
•	💼 Technology & Financial Services drive the highest won revenue
•	📈 Forecast Revenue: €429M with 56% forecast accuracy
•	⏳ Avg. Sales Cycle: ~131 days (Enterprise deals take longer but generate higher revenue per day)
•	🎯 Stage conversion and activity analysis highlight strong impact of workshops & demos on win rate

Through this challenge, I strengthened my skills in sales analytics, forecasting, KPI storytelling, and advanced Power BI visual design.
